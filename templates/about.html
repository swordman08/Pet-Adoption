Welcome to the Pet Adoption System

At the Pet Adoption System, we are dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need. Our mission is to connect shelters, adopters, and pets through a seamless and efficient platform. Whether you are looking to adopt a pet or manage shelter operations, we provide the tools and support to make it happen.

What We Offer

  • Browse pets available for adoption.
  • Manage shelter information and adoption applications.
  • Track pet health records and updates.
  • Seamlessly update and view photos of pets.
  • And much much more all thanks to our wonderful programers Decker and Art.

Our Story

This system was designed as part of the CPSC 408 course project by Decker and Art. Our vision is to make a meaningful impact in the lives of animals and the people who care for them. With user-friendly features and robust database management, we aim to simplify the adoption process and provide shelters with the tools they need to succeed.
